## Architecture: Tightly Coupled vs Loosely Coupled

<div class="architecture-comparison">
  <div class="arch-side tight-coupled">
    <div class="arch-icon">🔗</div>
    <h3>Tightly Coupled</h3>
    <div class="arch-content">
      <p><strong>High Dependencies</strong></p>
      <ul>
        <li>🚫 Teams block each other</li>
        <li>📞 Constant coordination needed</li>
        <li>🐌 Slower deployment cycles</li>
        <li>🔥 Cascading failures</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
  
  <div class="vs-divider">
    <span>VS</span>
  </div>
  
  <div class="arch-side loose-coupled">
    <div class="arch-icon">🧩</div>
    <h3>Loosely Coupled</h3>
    <div class="arch-content">
      <p><strong>Independent Teams</strong></p>
      <ul>
        <li>✅ Autonomous team work</li>
        <li>🚀 Faster feature delivery</li>
        <li>🛡️ Isolated failures</li>
        <li>📈 Better scalability</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
