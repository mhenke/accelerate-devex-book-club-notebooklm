## Quick Reference: Lean Management Practices

**WIP Limits:** Restrict number of concurrent work items to focus effort and expose bottlenecks

**Visual Management:** Dashboards and boards showing quality, work status, and blockers

**Feedback from Production:** Monitoring and observability drive continuous improvement

---

## Quick Reference: Burnout Prevention

**Systemic Solutions > Individual Training**

**Key Actions:**
- Reduce deployment pain through automation
- Remove organizational impediments
- Provide autonomy and decision-making authority
- Foster respectful, supportive work environment
- Give time and resources for learning/experimentation

---

## Quick Reference: Small Batch Benefits

**Risk:** Grows nearly quadratically with batch size
**Feedback:** Faster with smaller batches
**Recovery:** Easier to identify and fix issues
**Deployment:** More frequent with smaller changes
