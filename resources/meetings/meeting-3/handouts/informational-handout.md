
# Meeting 3: Informational Handout

## Key Topics

This handout provides a summary of the key topics for our third book club meeting.

### Chapter 7: Lean Management & Monitoring

*   **WIP Limits:** Use Work in Progress limits to improve flow and identify system bottlenecks.
*   **Visual Management:** Make work visible to everyone to track progress and quality.
*   **Lightweight Change Approval:** Replace heavyweight change approval processes with peer review.
*   **Proactive Monitoring:** Use system data to make informed business decisions.

### Chapter 8: Product Development

*   **Small Batches:** Work in small batches to reduce risk and get faster feedback.
*   **Customer Feedback:** Integrate customer feedback into the design process.
*   **Team Experimentation:** Empower teams to experiment and innovate.

### Chapter 9: Making Work Sustainable

*   **Deployment Pain:** Treat deployment pain as a signal of problems in the delivery process.
*   **Burnout Prevention:** CD practices can reduce burnout and improve work-life balance.
*   **Work-Life Balance:** Sustainable work practices are essential for long-term performance.

### Chapter 10: Employee Satisfaction & Engagement

*   **eNPS (Employee Net Promoter Score):** High performers are more likely to recommend their organization as a great place to work.
*   **Job Satisfaction:** Factors like autonomy, meaningful work, and good tools are key drivers of job satisfaction.
*   **Diversity:** Inclusive and diverse teams perform better and have better business outcomes.
