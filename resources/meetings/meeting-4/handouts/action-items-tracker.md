## Quick Reference: Transformational Leadership

**Five Characteristics:**
1. **Vision** - Clear, inspiring direction
2. **Intellectual Stimulation** - Challenge thinking
3. **Supportive Leadership** - Care and respect
4. **Personal Recognition** - Acknowledge contributions
5. **Role Modeling** - Lead by example

**Impact:** Leadership explains 18-50% of variance in key outcomes

## Quick Reference: Key Book Statistics

**High vs. Low Performers (2017):**
- **46x** more frequent deployments
- **440x** faster lead time
- **24x** faster recovery time
- **5x** lower change fail rate

**Other Key Findings:**
- CD enables **49%** time on new work vs. **38%**
- Trunk-based development: **2.3x** more likely in elite performers
- CABs: Negatively correlated with performance, NO stability improvement
- Toxic workplace: Predicts **60%** of burnout variance
- Small batches: Risk grows near-quadratically with batch size
