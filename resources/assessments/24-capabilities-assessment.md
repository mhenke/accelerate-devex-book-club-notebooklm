# 24 Capabilities Self-Assessment

## Purpose

This assessment helps you evaluate your team's current state across the 24 key capabilities identified in *Accelerate*. Use this to:
- Establish a baseline before starting the book club
- Identify high-impact improvement opportunities
- Track progress over time
- Prioritize which capabilities to focus on first

## Instructions

For each capability below, rate your team on a scale of 1-5:

- **1 = Not at all** - We don't do this or it's extremely rare
- **2 = Rarely** - We do this occasionally but inconsistently
- **3 = Sometimes** - We do this about half the time
- **4 = Often** - We do this most of the time with good consistency
- **5 = Always** - This is fully embedded in how we work

**Important:** Be honest! This is about understanding where you are now, not where you want to be. Lower scores indicate opportunities for improvement.

## Tips for Success

1. **Start Small:** Don't try to improve everything at once. Pick 1-2 capabilities to focus on.

2. **Go Deep Before Wide:** Fully implement one practice before moving to the next.

3. **Measure Progress:** Retake this assessment every 3-6 months to track improvement.

4. **Focus on Capabilities, Not Tools:** The book emphasizes what you can do, not what you buy.

5. **Build on Strengths:** Sometimes the fastest path forward is improving what's already working.

6. **Address Foundational Issues First:** Version control, CI, and culture often unlock other improvements.
